Values

Nuzen Herbal, India's leading brand in Hair and body care products, was formed in 2010 with the express aim of providing 100% natural alternatives to the existing market's mass produced synthetic cosmetics. Nuzen builds on the abundant wealth that Indian Ayurveda has to offer, harnessing the advances in modern science and production technology to bring affordable pharmaceutical grade herbal products to the people of India. Nuzen's products have found overwhelming acceptance in the Indian consumer market and have been endorsed by medical fraternity.


The Objective

Our endeavor has been to formulate safe, natural and innovative products to help people lead healthier, fulfilling lives. That NuZen products are endorsed by consumers and doctors worldwide stands testimony to the fact. NuZen has got approvals and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) Certification of AYUSH (Ayurvada, Unani, Sidda and Homoeopathy) from Andhra Pradesh state Government.

Leadership:

To encourage more as a team player rather than as a individual contributor.

Passion:

Working not only with applying your mind, but also you heart and compassion.

Accountability:
